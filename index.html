Lettuce

Lettuce is a popular leafy vegetable known for its crisp texture and nutritional value and is commonly used in salads, sandwiches and many other dishes.

Macronutrients include the three main components of nitrogen (N), phosphorus (P), and potassium (K), often referred to as NPK, as well as calcium (Ca), magnesium (Mg), and sulfur (S). Trace elements include iron (Fe), manganese (Mn), copper (Cu), zinc (Zn), boron (B), molybdenum (Mo) and chlorine (Cl)

Spinach

Learn about the specific requirements of spinach, from optimal light settings to the precise nutrient mix needed to produce robust, flavorful leaves.

Spinach requires a well-balanced nutrient regimen for optimal growth. Macronutrients include the primary components of nitrogen (N), phosphorus (P), and potassium (K) — collectively known as NPK — which are essential for healthy leaf development. Additionally, calcium (Ca), magnesium (Mg), and sulfur (S) are important for structural and enzymatic functions.
